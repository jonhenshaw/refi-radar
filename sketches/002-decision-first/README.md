## Variant: Decision First

### Design stance
Lead with whether the user should refinance, not with raw market data.

### Key choices
- Layout: recommendation card first, then rate stats and chart context.
- Typography: editorial, direct, human-readable status.
- Color: subtle signal colors instead of dense terminal visuals.
- Interaction: primary actions are Create target alert and Calculate savings.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: product value, personal usefulness, onboarding non-experts.
- Weak at: less data-dense for power users who just want charts.

### Best for
- Turning Refi Radar into a personal refinance assistant rather than a market monitor.

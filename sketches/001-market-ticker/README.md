## Variant: Market Ticker

### Design stance
A compact finance-app dashboard for repeat checking.

### Key choices
- Layout: one hero rate, chart above the fold, sources as dense rows.
- Typography: large but not card-dominating primary number.
- Color: dark X/Linear style with blue data accents.
- Interaction: tapping rows would open bottom-sheet history details.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: scanability, chart visibility, repeated daily use.
- Weak at: less dramatic than the current giant-card aesthetic.

### Best for
- Users who open the app often and want the latest market state in seconds.
